Adult Practice

All Are Welcome!

Asheville Aikikai invites men and women (ages 14 and up) for advanced and beginning practice. Beginners are welcome anytime.

If you are interested in observing or joining a class, please feel free to drop in 10 minutes prior to any class. Monthly dues are $60, with discounts for families and students.
